Parvej Husen Talukder

Bangladeshi poet and aspirng computer engineer

Md Parvej Husen Talukder, mostly known as Parvej Husen Talukder, is a Bangladeshi poet, young adult writer, internet entrepreneur, and former journalist. He founded Kavya Kishor International, the multilingual encyclopedia Bhikitia, and co-founded the wiki-based platform WikiGenius.
